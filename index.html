Some Work Samples

Coding in Python Language through Google Colab

This report presents an analysis of the major commodity prices from 2000-2022, including gold, palladium, nickel, Brent oil, natural gas, and wheat. Through the use of Python and visualizations, we aim to provide insights into the trends and patterns of these commodity prices over time, as well as identify any significant changes or anomalies.

Dashboarding through Spreadsheet or PowerBI

This report analyzes the international electric vehicle market using an Excel dataset to provide insights into the market's trends and patterns in sales, pricing, and demand. Our analysis is valuable for stakeholders in the automotive industry, governments, and other organizations looking to make informed decisions on electric vehicle production, sales, and infrastructure development. Through data visualization tools, we identify key market trends and provide actionable insights for stakeholders looking to capitalize on the growing popularity of electric vehicles.

Infographics on PWDs data

This report provides a series of infographics on persons with disabilities (PWDs) to showcase current trends and challenges related to disability inclusion and empowerment. Our infographics offer insights into disability prevalence, access to education and employment, health and well-being, and more. The information presented is useful for stakeholders in government, non-profit organizations, and the private sector as they seek to create more inclusive policies and practices. By using data visualization tools, we present complex information in a clear and accessible manner, making it easier for stakeholders to understand the challenges and opportunities facing PWDs today.

Potential New School Lands thru ArcGIS

This project involves using ESRI ArcGIS to analyze vector and raster data to identify potential new lands for building schools within the City of Greater Sudbury. The client has requested a final map that displays the results of the analysis. This project is useful for the environmental firm and the local government, as it will provide insights into potential areas for future development and growth.
